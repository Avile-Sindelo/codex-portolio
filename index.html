The Pig Game

Another 2-player game of dice.
Each player can ROLL a die multiple times, accumulating a current score, and can then HOLD. Upon Holding, the current score gets added to the player's global score, and its the next player's turn.
If you happen to roll a 1 in your multiple rolls though, you lose all your current score and its immediately the next player's turn.
The player whose GLOBAL score gets to 100 first, wins the game.
